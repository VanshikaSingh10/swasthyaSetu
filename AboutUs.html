Shri Shanta Swasthya Setu

उसका ध्यान रखने के लिए जो सबका ध्यान रखती है |

We made Shri Shanta Swasthya Setu because everyday women suffer from a lot of problems related to their health and well being. That's why we came up with a web space that not only provides women with proper knowledge and awareness but also helps them reach out to nearby health facilities, pharmacies and NGOs. We offer a centralised solution to women. Our name comes from a young lady who believed in helping other women for their upliftment. We imbibed these values of affection, empathy and help from her and here we are offering a one stop solution to all women. Our aim is to help all those women out there who need better guidance and awareness about their health.

"Serving the world to our world."
